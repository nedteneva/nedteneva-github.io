My PhD work focused on applying harmonic analysis to matrix factorizations and large graphs drawing on ideas from functional analysis, numerical linear algebra & randomized algorithms. I have experience working with biological datasets and have more recently applied Machine Learning to signal & audio processing tasks, text-based and natural language processing tasks.
